Design Notes

Why this concept: The goal is to show how manual, high-friction workflow steps can be translated into a structured internal product surface with clearer intake, approvals, and operational visibility.
What this demonstrates: Workflow design, review-state management, change visibility, exception handling, and clean internal UI execution in a controlled operational environment.
How I’d evolve it: Add persistent data storage, role-based permissions, evidence capture, exportable activity logs, and integration with upstream APIs or file-based inputs.
